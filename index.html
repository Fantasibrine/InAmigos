Who We Are

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization.

Headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we operate across India with a singular mission:

"Empowering lives through collective compassion and sustainable social action."

Our Projects & Causes

Project SEVA

"Serving Humanity with Compassion"

50,000+ meals and clothing items distributed to the underprivileged to ensure no one sleeps hungry.

Project BACHPANSHALA

"Nurturing Young Minds, Building Bright Futures"

Education support, digital literacy, and life skills for underprivileged children.

Project JEEV

"Empowering Lives, Spreading Compassion"

Animal welfare initiatives including rescues, protection, and feeding 50+ stray animals daily.

Project UDAAN

"Soaring Towards a Brighter Future"

Women empowerment via skill development, financial independence, and menstrual hygiene awareness.

Project PRAKRITI

"Plant for a Better Tomorrow"

20,000+ saplings planted alongside initiatives for eco-friendly agriculture and sustainability.

Project VIKAS

"Building Tomorrow's Workforce"

30,000+ interns trained in data ops, finance, digital marketing, social work, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-exempt?

Yes. InAmigos Foundation holds 80G and 12A certifications, making donations eligible for tax exemption under the Income Tax Act.

How can I volunteer?

Fill out the volunteer form at this link and our team will get in touch with you.

Where does InAmigos operate?

We operate across 28 states in India, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

Is InAmigos registered with the government?

Yes, we are Section 8, NITI Aayog, and CSR-1 registered, as well as IAF ISO 9001:2015 certified.
